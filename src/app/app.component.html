Sun position calculator on google maps

Predict size of shadows at different times of the day for google maps location.


Navigate to the plot where you plan to build a house and check how much sun your garden will get.
Use rectangle or polygon tool to draw area of your house/building on the map. You can specify heights (in meters) for each point.
The application will:
  • simulate casted shadow directly on the map
  • calculate sun shade length and angle on your parcel/land
  • predict shadow shape for any specified time, including both summer and winter solastice
  • display day duration, sunrise and sunset times for selected google maps location and date
  • display sun position: sun altitude and sun azimuth
