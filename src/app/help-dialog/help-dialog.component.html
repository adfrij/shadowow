Help

This application renders a shadow for specified date, time and location.

Choose or to select drawing mode, than click on the map to draw a custom shape.
You can specify height for each point.
Press b and n to modify time by 10 minutes.
Press c and v to modify date by one day.
Press ctrl and ← ↑ → ↓ to move shape by 0.1 meter. You can also move shape by dragging it.
Press ctrl + shift and ← → to rotate shape by 1 degree.
